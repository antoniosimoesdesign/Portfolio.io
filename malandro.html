MALANDRO
MALANDRO is a conceptual, fun, and relaxed brand for cooking bases. The other day I was looking at my portfolio and I realized it was lacking projects that tackled packaging design. With that in mind, I decided to focus on a project centered around packaging, this time featuring cooking bases such as tomato pulp, bechamel sauce, soy cream, and culinary cream. This project was quite fun to make because this time around I used a technique I had never thought of using before. First, I drew the laughing face doodles on a napkin in order to get that ink bleed effect, and then I proceeded to trace them digitally on the computer.
