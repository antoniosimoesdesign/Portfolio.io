COOL STICK OF THE DAY
Who has never went on a walk in a park or on the beach and found a really cool stick? Finding and collectin cool sticks on the beach is one of my earliest and dearest memories of my childhood.

At the begining of 2026 Portugal was hit by several storms that not only brought very high-speed winds but also very harsh sea conditions. After these storms, I went to the beach and was suprised when I found tons and tons of sticks that had been washed ashore by the rough seas. Seeing all those sticks gave me the idea to start an initiative where I would share a cool stick everyday. To see all the sticks I have shared so far, you can visit the project's website here.

Quick side note: in order to stay motivated during the making of this project, I listened to the song Stick by Dreamville multiple times.
