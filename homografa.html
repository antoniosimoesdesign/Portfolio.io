HOMÓGRAFA
HOMÓGRAFA is a hypothetical vinyl store based in Lisbon. Words are deemed homógrafas (in english homographs) if they are spelled the same way but have different pronunciations and meanings. This definition inspired the projects catchphrase "Different Meanings, Different Sounds". For this project I created a brand identity featuring a custom typeface. Made from the deconstruction of a speaker icon I carved out spaces in the letters to create this custom typography.

Since it's been a recurrent theme in my projects this time around I decided to develop a project around one of my biggest passion, Music. When I was younger, I used to listen to Pop, Rock and EDM. However, once I reached my teenage years I was introduced to Hip-Hop and I completly fell in love with the genre. Over time, my passion for this genre has also made me become interested in other genres of music, either through a collaboration or through the use of a sample in a song. With HOMÓGRAFA, I wanted to create a fictional, modern, relaxed vinyl store that provides vinyls from all genres of music and also promotes live performances.
