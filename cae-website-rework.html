CAE Website Rework
The Centro de Artes e Espectáculos, also known as CAE, is a cultural centre located in Figueira da Foz. With an agenda of events ranging from concerts and shows to plays and films, CAE plays a huge role in being a cultural force for the Central Region of Portugal.

While I was browsing the CAE website to find out about upcoming events, I noticed that it looked kinda dated so I decided to get inspired and give the CAE website a new and more modern look through this concept work. I decided to create color gradients and apply them to each section of the website. These gradients are meant to reference the wide range of events that this center hosts. Another detail I added was the presence of the color orange in all the gradients, due to the color of the bricks in the building's facade.
