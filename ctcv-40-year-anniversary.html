CTCV 40 Year Anniversary
As a centre that has been fostering innovation and developing technical and technological capabilities in the ceramics and glass industries for four decades, the Centro Tecnológico da Cerâmica e do Vidro (CTCV) marked this historic milestone by announcing a competition to develop a commemorative ceramic piece that embodies tradition, innovation, and a vision for the future. It was therefore fitting to design a solution rooted in the values of future vision and sustainability promoted by the centre.

At its core, the piece represents a trophy inspired by the shapes of the CTCV logo. Its base features 40 openings, symbolising the 40 years during which the centre has remained open to innovation. Extending through the piece, and drawing on the geometry of the logo, there is a protrusion that signifies both the journey undertaken over the past 40 years and the path that lies ahead. This piece was awarded 2nd place.
