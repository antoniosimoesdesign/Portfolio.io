POMADA
"Isto é cá uma pomada!" which translates to "What an ointment!" is what you say in portuguese when taste a very good wine. Based on this popular portuguese expression and our love and tradition for good wine I developed POMADA an hypothetical wine brand that produces: red, white and sparkling wine. The catchphrase "É uma pomada portuguesa com certeza!" which translates to "It's a portuguese ointment without a doubt!" pays homage to a classic fado song by Amália Rodrigues, Uma Casa Portuguesa.
