Pionés
As a fan of Stand Up Comedy, I often find myself searching for nearby shows. While doing this search I realised that there isn't a single platform displaying all the Stand Up shows in Portugal. More often than not, there are two or three major ticket selling platforms that have most of the shows, but not all, with the majority divided between these platforms. Some Stand Up Comedy tours even have tickets scattered across these platforms, which can make it difficult to find certain dates.

With this in mind, I decided to create Pionés. Pionés is portuguese for pins, you know, those little helpful pointy objects that helps us pin papers to corkboards. My intent with this platform was to create a space where ideally all the shows in Portugal were concentrated and displayed on the same space. Once you click to buy tickets for a show, you will be directed to the platform that actually sells them. With Pionés, you can also see the shows happening today, search for a particular comedian or show, and browse all the available shows.
