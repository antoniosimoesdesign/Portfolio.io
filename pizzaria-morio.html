Pizzaria Morio
Pizzaria Morio is a hypothetical Italian restaurant for football lovers. The color palette is inspired by the colors of the Italian flag, while the Hooligan Grotesk font is meant to resemble the numbers on the back of football jerseys. The slogan "Fazes cá falta!" is a playfull nod to when a player commits a foul and receives a red card in a match. In Portuguese, it can also mean “you are missed” or “we miss your company”.
