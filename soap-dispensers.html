Soap Dispensers
There are several everyday objects that often go unnoticed but are nevertheless crucial in our lives. One of these is the soap dispenser. Even without realizing it, we wash our hands several times a day (or at least we should), especially since the pandemic.

Through the API of the Instituto Português do Mar e da Atmosfera (IPMA), I extracted daily data from 12 coastal regions of Portugal. The parameters obtained from these coastal regions were: wave height, sea level, wave period, and sea surface temperature. Using Grasshopper, I applied these variables to influence the design of each soap dispenser, making each one unique and representative of a particular day in a given coastal region. Through this approach, this project combines generative design with ceramic 3D printing.
